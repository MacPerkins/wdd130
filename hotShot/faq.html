Frequently Asked Questions

What Type Of Target Do I Use?

There are two main ways to get targets for your shooting pleasure. Our app requires you to use our variety of targets for the time being as calculation of your score is depended on the predetermined scale and layout of our targets. Our website includes our free printable targets which you can use if you need some simple targets in a pinch. Through our website you can also order relatively inexpensive targets of varying size and styles. That way if you want a wider variety of targets for more long-range shots, you can have them!

What Is Included In The App?

Our app currently includes our easy to print targets for use at any range, as well as our target scanner. Once signed into your newly created account, you can save and view your previous targets and scores made over the history of your account. You can view your high-scores within our pre-created categories of rifle and pistol at long and short ranges. You can also narrow your scores down to the individual caliber of gun you are using, as well as the gun model. How Do I Share My scores And Progress?

So, you want to be a better shot than your friends? Sharing your personal scores to finally settle it is made easy through our app sharing. You can send your friends a link to your personal scores through text, email, or however you get around. If you want to post your personal shooting to your friends and followers on social media, you can do so easily through our cross platform sharing with Instagram and Facebook.
